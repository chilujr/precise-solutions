Our Services

Basic Accounting Functions

  • Financial Reporting: Accurate and timely preparation of financial statements to give you a clear picture of your businessâ€™s financial health.
  • Account Reconciliation: Regular reconciliation of accounts to ensure all financial records are precise and up-to-date.
  • Financial Analysis: Detailed analysis of financial data to help you make informed business decisions.

Bookkeeping

  • Daily Transaction Recording: Maintaining accurate records of all financial transactions on a daily basis.
  • Ledger Maintenance: Ensuring that all ledgers are maintained accurately and reflect the true financial position of your business.
  • Automation Solutions: Utilizing advanced bookkeeping software to streamline processes and reduce the chances of human error.

Tax Returns

  • VAT Returns: Preparation and submission of Value Added Tax returns to ensure compliance with local tax laws.
  • Income Tax Returns: Efficient handling of income tax filings, ensuring that all deductions and credits are accurately applied.
  • Tax Planning: Providing strategic tax planning advice to minimize liabilities and maximize savings.

Payroll Functions

  • PAYE Management: Comprehensive management of Pay As You Earn (PAYE) processes, ensuring accurate calculation and timely submission.
  • Salary Disbursement: Ensuring that employees are paid accurately and on time, every time.
  • Payroll Reporting: Generating detailed payroll reports to keep you informed of all payroll activities.

NAPSA and NHIMA Returns

  • NAPSA Returns: Efficient handling of NAPSA (National Pension Scheme Authority) returns, ensuring timely and accurate submissions to the pension fund. We manage all aspects of NAPSA compliance, from contributions to reporting.
  • NHIMA Returns: Expert management of NHIMA (National Health Insurance Management Authority) returns, ensuring that your business complies with Zambia's government medical aid requirements. We take care of all necessary paperwork and submissions to keep you compliant.
